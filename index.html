How To Play

  • First the Player with the Symbol starts and puts their symbol anywhere in the 3x3 matrix.
  • Then the Player with the Symbol get the chance and puts their symbol in any of the vacant spaces in the 3x3 matrix.
  • Every Player plays the alternative move.
  • Any player who have all of their symbols in any of the row/column/diagonal first, wins the game.
  • This game is not at all luck based, gradually as you play more and more you will get to know the best moves to play.
